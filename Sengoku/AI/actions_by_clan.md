## Retainers Actions
### Samurai - Elite Knight (#Army 1)
A1: Place *near* or Cover a card of your
*Clan*, then Kill *1* *unprotected* *adjacent* card or Remove 1 Token  
A2: During an opponent's turn,
Block their *Special*'s card action.
[The other player can use another card]

### Ashigaru - Light Infantry (#Army 2)
A1: 
Place *near* a card of your *Clan*
Kill up to 2 *adjacent* *unprotected* *Number* or *face-down* cards  
A2: Draw *4* cards, Keep *1* and
Return the others to the top
or bottom of the deck

### Chajin - Tea Master (#Power 1)
A1: Draw 2 new cards [max 1 of this Chajin action per turn]  
A2: Draw and Play 1 card right away.
If the card is a *Special* then can
Add +*2* to the effect [max *3*]

### Sohei - Warrior Monk (#Power 2)
A1: Place on top of another Sohei
or *unprotected* *Numbered*
or *face-down* card of any *Clan*  
A2: Retrieve the top *1* card from
the discard pile into your hand

### Shinobi - Spy Assassin (#Mercenary 1)
A1: Kill and Remove from the map
1 *Special* + any Token on it
or 1 *face-down* card 
A2: Spy on a player's hand, Pick *2*
cards to Discard, then Draw *text+1*
extra card at the end of the turn 

### Ronin - Masterless Warrior (#Mercenary 2)
A1: Replace 1 *unprotected* [non Ronin]
card and Return it to the owner,
Kill or Return the rest of the stack  
A2: Steal up to *2* Tokens from
*Number* or *Retainer* cards
[max 1 per Player, rest from resources]
