# Retainers Actions

Compact helper only. If anything here conflicts with `AI/v25/RulesV25-Final.md`, follow `AI/v25/RulesV25-Final.md`.
Text is normalized from `data.json` by expanding card tags into the printed wording.
Examples: `special` -> `Retainer`, `numbered` -> `Numbered`, `covered` -> `face-down`, `protected` -> `protected`.

### Samurai - Elite Knight (#Army 1)
A1: Place *near your chain* or cover your *Clan* card, then kill *1* *unprotected* *adjacent* card or remove *1* Token or *1* Market  
A2: During an opponent's turn, cancel a declared *Retainer* card action before it resolves; discard that card too  
Note: Samurai cannot block Trap effects

### Ashigaru - Light Infantry (#Army 2)
A1: Place *near your chain* or cover any own-clan *unprotected* *Numbered* card, then kill up to *2* *adjacent* *unprotected* *Numbered* or *face-down* cards  
A2: Draw *4* cards, keep *1*, and return the others together to the top or bottom of the deck

### Chajin - Tea Master (#Power 1)
A1: Draw *2* cards [max *1* Chajin action per turn]  
A2: Draw *1* card; if it is a *Retainer* and you play it right away, its bonus becomes *3*

### Sohei - Warrior Monk (#Power 2)
A1: Place on a Sohei, *unprotected* *Numbered* card, or *face-down* card of any *Clan*  
A2: Retrieve the top *1* card from your discard pile into your hand

### Shinobi - Spy Assassin (#Mercenary 1)
A1: Remove from the map *1* *Retainer* and any Token on it, or *1* *face-down* card  
A2: Spy on a player's hand, choose *2* cards to discard, then draw *+1* card at the end of the turn  
Trap: affects whoever revealed it

### Ronin - Masterless Warrior (#Mercenary 2)
A1: Place on your own or any *Clan*'s *unprotected* top [non-Ronin] card, replacing it; return that card, then discard or return the rest of the stack  
A2: Steal up to *2* Tokens from cards of other *Clans* [max *1* per Clan, rest from supply]  
Trap: from the revealer first when applicable
