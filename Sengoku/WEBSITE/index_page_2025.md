## 04 - Sengoku Period Introduction

### What Was Visible

A dark section with a large illustrated samurai figure centered above a dense historical text block.

The content focused heavily on the historical Sengoku period before clearly explaining the game experience.

### Text

The Sengoku period(戦国時代, Sengoku Jidai, "Warring States period") was a period in the Japanese history of near-constant civil war and social upheaval from 1467-1615.

Dominated by the most charismatic and legendary daimyo such as Oda Nobunaga, Toyotomi Hideyoshi, Akechi Mitsuhide and Tokugawa Ieyasu, this was probably, the most fascinating and controversial period in Japanese history.

Everyone, from the humble peasant to the Ashigaru, from the Samurai to the Shogun, was fighting, making pacts or betraying to prevail over the rivals seeking to conquer the entire nation province by province.

Prepare to embark on a riveting journey, where you will navigate the intricate web of power dynamics, strategic choices, and relentless ambition, all with the ultimate goal of emerging victorious amidst this sweeping saga of conquest.

### What Was Visible

A dark section with a small red cube image above a large centered text block.

### Text

Intensive Games is an independent board game design team based in London, UK. With a deep love for all types of games, we are dedicated to creating experiences that bring joy and excitement to players worldwide.

Speaking of myself, my name is Gianluca, and my passion for board games has been with me since my early days.

I've always enjoyed exploring new ideas and sharing this enthusiasm with my family and close friends. The concept of Sengoku emerged during a lively discussion around the gaming table with my daughter, and we immediately sensed the potential for something very special.

As we continued to refine and iterate the game, the positive feedback from playtests drove our determination to see Sengoku come to life.

It has been an incredible journey, blending our fascination with Japanese history and the magic of board games into a unique creation.

As we approach the final stages of development, I invite you to join us on this thrilling adventure.
